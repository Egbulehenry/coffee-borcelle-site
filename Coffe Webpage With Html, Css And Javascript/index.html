Espresso Coffee

Espresso Coffee is a concentrated form of coffee served in small, strong shots and is the base for many coffee drinks. It's made by forcing hot water under high pressure through finely-ground coffee beans, resulting in a rich, bold flavor with a signature crema on top.

How to Prepare Espresso Coffee

  1. Grind 18-20 grams of fresh coffee beans to a fine consistency.
  2. Tamp the ground coffee evenly into the portafilter with about 30 pounds of pressure.
  3. Attach the portafilter to the espresso machine and start the extraction.
  4. Run hot water (about 195-205°F) through the coffee for 25-30 seconds to yield a 2-ounce shot.
  5. Serve immediately, topped with the natural crema, and enjoy!
